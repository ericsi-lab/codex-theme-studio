Files under `docs/examples/real/` are factual screenshots of the official ChatGPT desktop
application with Theme Studio active. They are included for compatibility demonstration and remain

new-task and settings views. Only account avatar, username and email are masked before pixels are
written to disk; project names, workspace labels, the full sidebar and application structure remain
visible and uncropped.
